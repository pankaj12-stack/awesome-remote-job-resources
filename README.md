## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Landing Remote Work

### Before Applying

1. **Verify it's truly remote** - Check the company's remote policy
2. **Research the company** - Look up reviews on Glassdoor, Blind
3. **Check timezone requirements** - Can you work during their hours?
4. **Understand the contract type** - Employee vs contractor vs freelance
5. **Know the legal implications** - Taxes, work permits, etc.

### Your Application

1. **Highlight remote experience** - Even if it's just school/personal projects
2. **Show self-motivation** - Remote work requires discipline
3. **Emphasize communication skills** - Critical for distributed teams
4. **Demonstrate async work ability** - Not everyone will be online together
5. **Include timezone** - Make it easy for them to schedule interviews

### Interview Preparation

1. **Test your tech** - Camera, microphone, internet connection
2. **Prepare your space** - Professional background, good lighting
3. **Have backup plans** - What if your internet dies?
4. **Ask about remote culture** - How does the team collaborate?
5. **Clarify expectations** - Work hours, communication frequency, etc.
